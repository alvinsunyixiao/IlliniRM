  • About Us
    • We are a group of young engineers who believes that robotics will be the powerful way to build the future. As the development of the robotic technology becomes much faster and more diverse, it is the time for us to start preparing for the future. At the meanwhile, DJI introduces the RoboMaster competition (learn more about RoboMaster). Without any hesitation, we decide to register for the competition. In October, 2017, on the U of I campus, a team of engineering students form the Illini RoboMaster. Being part of the RoboMaster competition is our way to verify our skill and ability for the future robotic industry.

    Building a robot requires knowledge from many different fields especially areas like electrical engineering, mechanical engineering and computer science. Illini RoboMaster consists of technology department and management department. We divide up our technology team based on the area our teammates focus on: We have ECE team, CS team, and ME team. We also have professor from ECE department as our advisor. We welcome people from different engineering areas who are interested in building robotics for the future. There is no limitation for what major you are or which graduation class you are coming from. We would like to have future scientists, innovators and entrepreneurs to be part of our team.

    RoboMaster is just the beginning of our journey. We will never be satisfied to be staying in one spot, but rather than that we hope one day we will build robotics to benefit our society.
